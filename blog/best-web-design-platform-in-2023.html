Best Web Design Platform in 2023

As a web design agency based in Australia, we often get asked, "What is the best web design platform?" The truth is, there is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question. It depends on your business's specific needs and requirements. However, we have worked with several web design platforms and can provide insight into some of the best options available.

By Zach Morse

Published April 28, 2023

WordPress

WordPress is one of the most popular web design platforms out there. It's open-source and free to use, which makes it an attractive option for many businesses. WordPress is highly customizable and has a large library of themes and plugins, which allows for endless design possibilities. It's also great for SEO, as it has built-in features that help optimize your website for search engines.

We have worked with WordPress on many projects and can attest to its effectiveness as a web design platform. We especially love how easy it is to use, which allows our clients to update their websites themselves without needing to contact us for assistance.

Shopify

Shopify is a platform specifically designed for eCommerce websites. It's a great option for businesses that want to sell products online. Shopify is easy to use, and it has many features that make managing an eCommerce website a breeze. Some of these features include inventory management, payment processing, and shipping options.

We have worked with Shopify on several eCommerce projects, and we've found it to be an excellent option for businesses that want to sell products online. The platform is easy to use, and it has a large library of themes and apps that allow for endless design possibilities.

Squarespace

Squarespace is a popular platform for businesses that want a sleek and modern-looking website. It has a drag-and-drop interface, which makes it easy to use, even for those who have no coding experience. Squarespace has several beautiful templates to choose from, which makes designing a website a breeze.

We have worked with Squarespace on several projects, and we love how easy it is to use. The platform is great for businesses that want a simple, clean, and modern-looking website.

Wix

Wix is another popular platform for web design. It's similar to Squarespace in that it has a drag-and-drop interface and many beautiful templates to choose from. Wix is also easy to use, even for those who have no coding experience.

We have worked with Wix on several projects, and we've found it to be a great option for businesses that want a simple, easy-to-use website. However, we've found that it can be a bit limited in terms of design flexibility, so it may not be the best option for businesses that want a highly customized website.

Webflow

Webflow is a visual website builder that allows designers to create responsive websites without needing to know how to code. It has a drag-and-drop interface, like Wix and Squarespace, but it also offers a more customizable experience for designers who want more control over their website's design. Webflow also offers a CMS (content management system) for easy content updates.

We have worked with Webflow on several projects and have found it to be a great option for businesses that want a highly customized website but don't have the technical expertise to build one from scratch. The platform has a bit of a learning curve, but once you get the hang of it, it offers a powerful design experience.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are many great web design platforms available, each with its own unique features and benefits. WordPress, Shopify, Squarespace, Wix, and Webflow are just a few of the many options available. When choosing a platform, it's essential to consider your business's specific needs and requirements. Do you need an eCommerce platform? Do you want a simple and easy-to-use platform or one that's highly customizable? Do you have coding experience or prefer a drag-and-drop interface? Answering these questions will help you determine which platform is best for you.
