FAQ'S

What services do we offer?

Zach Morse Media offers various marketing techniques including website design, development, hosting and Search Engine Optimisation (SEO).




How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline for building a website depends on the complexity of the site. A basic website can be done in a few weeks. While a complex website may take several months.




How much does a website cost?

The cost of a website depends on the complexity of the site. A basic website can cost a few thousand dollars. While a complex website can cost tens of thousands of dollars.




What is search engine optimization (SEO)?

SEO is the process of optimising your website to rank higher in search engine results pages (SERPs). This involves improving your website's content, structure, and acquiring quality backlinks so it appeals to search engines like Google.




How long does it take to see results from SEO?

SEO is a long-term strategy, well developed sites can usually see results in a couple months while new sites can take several months to a year before results can be seen. We help you set realistic goals and expectations for our clients SEO campaigns.
